Music Recommender

Personalized artist recommendations using Spotify, Last.fm, and Bluesky

Pipeline

  • Connects to your Spotify account to see what you've been listening to
  • Builds your personal music taste profile based on genres and descriptive tags
  • Finds candidate artists similar to ones you listen to then expands outward
  • Ranks new artists by how well they match your taste profile
  • Factors in which artists people are currently talking about on Bluesky
  • Combines all three signals into a single ranked recommendation list
⚠ Access restriction This app is in Spotify development mode. Personal listening data can be retrieved only from accounts explicitly added to the allowlist. If your account has not been added, the pipeline will still run but recommendations will not reflect your personal taste.
Connect with Spotify

You will be redirected to Spotify to authorize read access to your top artists, top tracks, and saved library.
