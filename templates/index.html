Music Recommender

Personalized artist recommendations from your Spotify history

How it works

  • Builds a taste profile from your genre + tag distributions (TF-IDF, cosine similarity)
  • Expands a two-hop similar-artist graph via Last.fm
  • Pulls cultural buzz from Bluesky posts and Album of the Year
  • Combines all three signals into a weighted final score
  • Runs a Naive Bayes classifier as an alternative ranking
Connect with Spotify

You'll be redirected to Spotify to authorize access to your listening history.
