Music Recommender

Personalized artist recommendations using Spotify, Last.fm, and Bluesky

Pipeline

  • Fetches your Spotify top artists and saved library (live, personal)
  • Builds a genre and tag profile from Last.fm artist tags weighted by playcount
  • Expands a two-hop similar-artist graph via Last.fm's similarity API
  • Scores candidates using TF-IDF cosine similarity against your tag centroid
  • Pulls cultural buzz from Bluesky post engagement with time decay
  • Combines taste, social, and buzz into a weighted final score (0.4 / 0.3 / 0.3)
⚠ Access restriction This app is in Spotify development mode. Only accounts explicitly added to the allowlist can retrieve personal listening data. If your account has not been added, the pipeline will still run but recommendations will not reflect your personal taste — contact the project team to request access.
Connect with Spotify

You will be redirected to Spotify to authorize read access to your top artists, top tracks, and saved library. No data is stored after your session ends.
