Multi-signal music recommender

Discover music mapped to your taste graph

Connect your Spotify account. SoundGraph pulls your listening history, builds a taste profile via TF-IDF and cosine similarity, expands through a Last.fm artist similarity graph, and fuses cultural buzz signals from Bluesky and Album of the Year into a ranked recommendation list.

Connect with Spotify
How it works
Pipeline 01

User Profile

Builds genre & tag distributions from your Spotify top artists and Last.fm scrobbles using TF-IDF weighting.

Pipeline 02

Social Graph

Expands a two-hop similar-artist graph via Last.fm, scoring candidates by seed overlap and match strength.

Pipeline 03

Cultural Buzz

Scrapes Bluesky posts and AOTY reviews, computing engagement-weighted buzz with VADER sentiment analysis.
